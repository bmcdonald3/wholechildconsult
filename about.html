About Mary

Mary McDonald is a licensed teacher and academic coach for children and adolescence who want to increase their independence and intrinsic motivation in and out of school. Mary has worked in public schools as a high school Special Education teacher and with families of kids with disabilities for nearly ten years.

In her work, she helps kids overcome the academic and executive functioning barriers that prevent them from reaching their goals. Mary challenges kids to get to know their strengths and weaknesses, communicate their needs, reflect on their performance, and set ambitious goals for themselves.
